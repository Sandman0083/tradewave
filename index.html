About Us

TRADEWAVE CARRIERS PRIVATE LIMITED is a leading logistics firm specializing in transportation and end-to-end customs clearing and forwarding services across India and Bangladesh borders. Our solutions are built to ensure hassle-free movement of cargo, with complete regulatory compliance and operational transparency.

With years of experience in EXIM trade and logistics, our mission is to make international shipping fast, reliable, and economical. We are strategically positioned near Petrapole — India’s largest land port — making us the first choice for Indo-Bangladesh trade operations.

Our Services

  • Customs Clearing: Documentation, compliance, and clearance for imports and exports via land, air, and sea ports.
  • Transportation: Door-to-door cargo transport, container movement, and loose cargo across India and Bangladesh.
  • Forwarding Services: Freight booking, packaging, insurance, and regulatory consultation.
  • Border Handling: Specialized movement through congested land ports like Petrapole with minimum delays.
  • Consulting: Guidance on HS Codes, documentation requirements, duty structures, and trade incentives (DEPB, Drawback, etc.).

Our Working Process

We follow a well-defined and transparent approach:

  1. Step 1: Receive shipment details and documentation from the client.
  2. Step 2: Verify customs eligibility, prepare bill of entry/shipping bill, and file with ICEGATE.
  3. Step 3: Coordinate with transporters, warehouses, and port officials.
  4. Step 4: Ensure timely clearance, dispatch, and delivery with real-time updates to the client.
